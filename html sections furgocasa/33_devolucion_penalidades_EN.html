⚠️ Mandatory drop-off: clean van and empty tanks

At the agreed time, the vehicle must be returned as per the rental agreement. It is mandatory to return it clean inside and with all tanks emptied (fresh water, grey water and toilet / black water), unless FURGOCASA states otherwise in writing.

  • Fresh water: tank emptied (or level as recorded on handover).
  • Grey water: tank emptied.
  • Toilet / black water: emptied (cassette / tank depending on model).
  • Fuel: same level as at the start (handover report). If supplied full, return full.
  • Time: return in the agreed slot; keys handed to FURGOCASA staff.

If you exceed the drop-off time, you may have to wait until the next opening slot and late fees below may apply.

Fees if requirements are not met (VAT included)

Each line is separate: several breaches may be charged together.

⏰ Late return / appointment

Punctuality matters: our team schedules each handover. Waiting time due to lateness is billable.

  • First hour late €40
  • Each additional hour €20
  • Maximum per day (lateness) 2 rental days
Important: failing to attend an appointment without notice may be treated as cancellation of the handover service with no refund of amounts already due, as per contract.

💡 Tip

Running late? Contact Furgocasa as soon as possible — you can often extend the rental at the daily rate, which is usually cheaper than cumulative late fees. Subject to availability if another booking follows yours.
